The Venue

Hotel Contempo is the perfect spot for a gathering of modern artists. Not only are the conferece rooms halls decked with breathaking contemporary art and sculptures, but individual rooms are as unique as the renowned artist who were commissioned to decorate them. From the Ross Monroe Purple suite filled wall to wall with painting in his pallete of violet and lavander to the Test Lessinger Sculpted Universe suite with dozens of original sculptures including the bronze-casted toilet visitors are sure to be intrigued and comforted during their stay at Hotel Contempo. For those who opt to stay at another location where is no shortage of hotels in Downtown Seattle. Ranging from shabby chic to the ultimate in sophistication

Phillips of Bell Town

Situated among the hip, youthrul culture of Downtown Seattle. Phillips of Beltown is the place to be any time of the day or night. Choose from Jazz and Rock music at various music venues and shop until you drop at an assortment of thrift stores and upscale boutiques. The hotel itself is an historical gem with architecture archivements in every beam, brick and support

The Other Renaissance Hotel

Hotel Founder Henry Chasings had a love of otters, having been raised in Alaska village where otters played out his back door. As his tribute to the sea creatures of his early days. Henry was insistent upon having an otter in every hall wall and room inside the Other Renaissance Hotel.

Gwendoline's Fancy

In the heart of West Edge district in Seattle. Gwendoline's Fancy named after a Navy Submarine that got lost at sea in 1910 is a central landing place for history buffs who can immerse themselves in the Museum of History located in the hotel mezzanine. For those travelers who aren't into history there are plent of other nearby sights to keep them entertained including Pike Place Market and the Seattle Art Museums.

Featured Artist

The Roux Academy selects approximately 200 distincs pieces of contemporary art for display in thir collections exhibit. Nine individuals are granted his or her own exhibit hall to display entire collections or themed pieces. Each Featured Artist has an opportunity to speak at the conference to share his or her vision, perspective, and techniques with conference attendees.
